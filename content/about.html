ABOUT

Born in 1999, Mael (Marko Ludaic) is a Serbian singer-songwriter who combines the elements of classical music, r&b, soul and trap to create a new unique sound. His first single "Volja" was released in January and it was the title-track of his EP. Marko plays three musical instruments. He has finished music school for piano, violin and opera signing and has been producing music since 2016.
